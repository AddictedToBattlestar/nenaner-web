Privacy policy for the College Relations application

Data collection

This application stores all registrant information locally on the device where the application is installed. The owners of this device will never collect this information stored on your device.

Third party

This application does not share any information with any third party.

Data retention

As the data you accumulate is stored locally on your device, this data will remain there until you clear this data within the application. Additionally, removal of the application on your device will in turn delete the application data stored on that device.
